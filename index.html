Its Cee (Senate Ramoroke)

Born in South Africa and raised in both South Africa and Lesotho, Its Cee is a solo artist with a passion for music, songwriting, performance, and creative design. Currently studying Broadcast and Journalism at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, Its Cee began his musical journey in 2014 under the name Yung Cee. In 2023, he rebranded as Its Cee, continuing to evolve his sound and artistry.

A skilled songwriter and performer, Its Cee also excels in design using Adobe apps. He is known for his energetic stage presence and creative vision, making waves in the Southern African music scene.
